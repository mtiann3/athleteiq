AthleteIQ is a fitness app designed to help users achieve their health and fitness goals by providing personalized workout plans, diet recommendations, and exercises made to their specific needs. Users can enter information about their current health status, fitness goals, and the app will use this information to create a customized plan that will help them improve their strength and athletic performance. The app will also offer exercises and activities that are specific to the sports or activities that the user participates in. Overall, this app is the perfect tool for anyone looking to improve their fitness, strength, and overall athletic performance.

Made by Mike Iannotti with ReactJs and Tailwindcss, 2023
